Anansi Boys

What is it about?

Charlie, a downtrodden London man with unenthusiastic wedding plans, learns his dad had died, so he goes to Florida for the funeral. After that, he learns that he has a brother, and to get his attention, he should talk to a spider. Charlie, at first ignores this advice, then gets drunk sometime later, and summons his brother by whispering to a spider. What happens next?
